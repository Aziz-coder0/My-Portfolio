Computer Ethics and Copyright

what is Computer Ehtics?

Computer ethics is the set of commonly agreed principles that govern the use of computers.
Like ethics more generally,computer ethics is essentially asit of philosophical guidelines or moral standard that aim to imfluence behavior and prevent harm.
Every cyber citizen should think about and adhere to these ideals or "commandments"while online or using a computer.

The following are the Ten Commandments of Computer.

1)Thou shalt not use a computer to harm other people.

2)Thou shalt not interfer with other people's computer work.

3)Thou shalt not snoop around in other people's computer files.

4)Thou shalt not use a computer to steal.

5)Thou shalt not use a computer to bear false witness.

6)Thou shalt not copy or use proprietary software for which you have not paid.

7)Thou shalt not use other people's computer resources without authorization or proper compensation.

8)Thou shalt not approprate other people's intellectual output.

9)Thou shalt think about the social consequences of the program you are writing are the system you are designing.

10)Thou shalt always use a computer in ways that ensure consideration and respect for your fellow humans.

What is copyright?

Copyright is a type of intellectual property that protects original works of authorship as soon as an author fixes the work in a tangible form of expression. In copyright law,there are lot of different types of works,including paintings,photographs,illustrations,musical compositions,sound recording,computer programs,books,poems,blog posts,movies,architectural works,plays,and so much more!
